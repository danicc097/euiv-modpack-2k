[h1][b]Welcome to Peace Treaties Expanded[/b][/h1]
This mod adds new peace treaties and Casus Bellis to add more variety to peace offers.

[h1]Currently Added[/h1]
Currently [i]Peace Treaties Expanded[/i] adds the following peace options. Some require a special CB or have minor requirements to show up in the peace treaty window (e.g. require a specific army size).
Additional information about each treaty as well as the added Casus Bellis can be found [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/2615504872/2961670721745264890/]here[/url].
